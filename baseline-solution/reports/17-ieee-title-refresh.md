# IEEE Title Refresh

Regeneration was necessary.

The earlier title, `Can AI See Structure in Schematics? A Comparative Evaluation of Multimodal Models for Circuit Diagram Understanding and Reconstruction`, was broader and more exploratory than the current manuscript. The paper now has a clearer emphasis on:

- multi-agent benchmarking,
- reconstruction from schematic images,
- and dual simulator-oriented outputs through Falstad and LTSpice.

## Final Title

Multi-Agent Schematic Reconstruction from Images: A Comparative Benchmark Using Falstad and LTSpice Artefacts
